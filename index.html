Claim

The black box has a mechanism that divides water that inevitably drains into the output.

Evidence

The Black box Took water in and gave out water most tests resulted in less out but some resulted in more out.

Reasoning

Part 1

Output- a tube in the middle of the box Input- a funnel of the top of box Black Box- A Sealed Black Box with an input and output

Part 2

It shows me that there is a mechanism in the box because not all the water is returned at once

Part 3

If there was not a mechanism that divides water that later drains into an output, our observations of the real mystery black box would have been different. Specifically, there would be a direct correlation between input and output.
